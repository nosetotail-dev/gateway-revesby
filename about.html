About Us

Gateway Capital is an Australian based investment management business focused on creating core assets in the industrial and logistics sectors through active management and development.

Gateway Capital partners with institutional investors to drive value from real estate through defined investment strategies to deliver outsized returns.

Our fully integrated management platform covering capital transactions, investment management, asset management, property management, redevelopment and finance provides strong foundations for the execution of our strategies. We believe this provides a unique advantage in executing strategies on underutilized assets and sites.

Our highly skilled and experienced team have a strong track record of acquiring, developing and adding value to assets for tenants and investors. Our focus on tenant relationships ensures we can evolve with the fluid market dynamics to provide a range of space solutions tor meet the diverse occupier demand, whilst delivering strong outcomes for our stakeholders.

Environmental Social Governance

We are committed to investing and operating responsibly to drive and influence the greatest possible positive impact for our people, the planet and the community.

ESG considerations are core to our values, with responsible thinking built into the fabric of how we do business; all decisions are executed with the intention of driving positive impact and promoting responsible practices. We work to leave our communities and the planet in a better position than when we began, facilitating prosperity for future generations.

Environmental

Understanding and managing the impact that our organisation has on the environment in which we operate

Social

Recognising how the operations of our business effects and impacts all stakeholders, including our staff, tenants, partners and the broader community

Governance

Establishing and maintaining a framework for responsible decision making to consider the rights, responsibilities and broader interests of our stakeholders

Focus Areas

Carbon Footprint

Tracking our resource use and understanding our carbon footprint with a view that our organisational strategy will drive reductions in carbon over the coming 3 years

Climate Impact

Monitoring the impacts of climate change and associated market and regulatory changes on our assets, as well as the contribution of our investments on climate change and the planet.

ESG Collaboration

Pursue, where feasible and mutually agreed, green leasing arrangements with our tenants to drive and realise mutual benefit across our properties through strong ESG collaboration
