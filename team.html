Executive Team

Gateway Capital is an Australian based investment management business focused on creating core assets in the industrial and logistics sectors through active management and development.

Stuart Dawes

Chief Executive Officer and Co-Founder

Stuart is a co-founder of Gateway Capital and has over 23 years’ experience in the property industry in Australia and Europe working with institutional capital on real estate investment strategies. Before establishing Gateway Capital, Stuart was the Managing Director and CEO of ASX listed Propertylink Group, leading the growth in the Investment Management business to over $2 billion of assets under management before guiding the Company to an IPO in 2016.
