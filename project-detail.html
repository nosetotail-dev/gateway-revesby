Highly sought after Laverton North industrial precinct

Gilbertson Road, provides a fully leased site in the well positioned in the highly sought after Laverton North industrial precinct. The 4.4ha landholding boats excellent access to Western Ring Road, West Gate Freeway, located less than 10 minutes from the Intermodal the site is positioned within more than 20 million square meters of industrial GFA within a 30 minute drive time.

The site provides 24,127 sqm of lettable area across 8 separate industrial office and warehouse facilities ranging in size from 2,650 – 3,082 sqm with each providing full drive through access.

The asset is positioned to benefit from major infrastructure projects which will provide greater connectivity to the offering and easier access to Port of Melbourne including the Port Rail Shuttle, West Gate Tunnel Project, Western Intermodal Freight Terminal and more.
